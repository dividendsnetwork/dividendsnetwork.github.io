Quick Start
1. Place a bid (costs 0.1 eth) to try to become leader of all of the pots
2. 1/3 of your bid goes to each pot (hour, day, week)
3. Each bid gives you a different random number between 1 - 1,000,000 for each pot
4. If you manage to get a higher number than the leader of a pot, you become the new leader!
5. If you hold leader for the pot length (hour, day, or week), you win half the pot
