Tokenomics

How does it work?

DVN Token is the base token for the DIVIDENDS Network ecosystem. The token is self-sustainable, minting upon purchase and burning upon selling.

Essentially, the total supply is based solely on the buying and selling transactions with in the ecosystem.

FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most commonly asked questions regarding the DIVIDENDS Network

Who has control of the project?
The project is fully decentralized on the Blockchain. No one will have control over the project. Token holders will control their tokens/eth and the smart contracts will work independently of the developers.
Where will the OTC proceeds go?
90% of the OTC will go towards the funding of the project. This includes Staking funds, token buy backs and locked liquidity. The remaining 10% will be allocated to the development team.
Should I join the presale OTC?
Naturally, the OTC presale gives participants an upperhand in terms of positioning with the platform. Whether or not you "should" get in is based solely at your discretion.
Will the team own a percentage of tokens?
Yes, the team will hold a portion of tokens however majority of it will be used for marketing purposes. The team makes a percentage of all buys and sells and therefore does not need to be greedy and also own tokens to sell.
What is the purpose of buybacks?
When the token price drops, buybacks are essential to helping with price recovery and all purchased tokens will be added to the SMART Staking dApp.
